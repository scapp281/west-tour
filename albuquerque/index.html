Albuquerque Introduction

Located in west central New Mexico between the Sandia Mountains and the Rio Grande. Albuquerque is the largest city in the state and one of the nation's fastest growing cities. Albuquerque was named after the Duke of Alburquerque, a viceroy of New Spain. Today, Albuquerque is a center for timber, livestock and farming, and a growing area for high-technology industries. Other industries important to the economy include health services, banking and tourism. Because of Albuquerque's high technology and military industries, it is home to a large number of people with doctorate degrees. Therefore, the city ranking in the top ten nationally in quantity of PhDs per capita for larger cities.


Whether you're into adventures, culture, history, outdoor or just in need of some new and unique scenery, Albuquerque offers exciting attractions for all ages and interests, anytime of the year. In the historic section of "The Duke City", ancient Indian and Spanish cultures come to life through old churches, quaint shops and piazzas, winding brick paths and adobe benches. Visitors can spend a whole day making their way through more than 100 stores, boutiques and galleries that sell Southwestern goods, as well as unique products, from around the world.


During the winter holiday season, the city is adorned with small lit candles. Thousands come to stroll through the streets, listen to live music and celebrate. It is truly a sight to see. But the history doesn't stop there. The Indian Pueblo Cultural Center and the Petroglyph National Monument offer a glimpse of old Indian customs and art. Plenty of families suitable activites will find at the Albuquerque Biological Park, which features a zoo, aquarium and botanic garden.


Outdoor types will find ample opportunities for hiking, biking and golf. After working up an appetite, head to any number of fine restaurants that serve up a choice of hot or even hotter New Mexican cuisine. To top it all off, take a ride on the Sandia Peak Aerial Tramway for spectacular views of all this vibrant city has to offer.
