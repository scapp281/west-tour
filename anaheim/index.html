Anaheim Introduction

City of Anaheim received two 2014 Golden Hub of Innovation Awards. Public Engagement Award recognizes new and innovative ways to engage the public. Energy/Environmental Quality Award recognizes innovation in energy efficiency and reduced energy consumption, which promotes sustainable living or compliance with environmental regulations.


Under Disney influence, Anaheim,formerly a sleepy suburban town, today is a vibrant tourist center with fine dining, shopping, festivals, a myriad of attractions and a vast array of accommodations.Beside Disneyland, Downtown Disney is a great place for adults and families. There are variety of store, restaurants, and nighclubs. For kids, there are Build-A-Bear Workshop and Lego Imagination Center, tools providing for them to create their creative creatures. For food, Rainforest Cafe is the place to be. Rainforest Cafe has the wild tropical rainforest environment with waterfalls, lush vegetation, and indigenous creatures.


For a change of pace, check out the Outlets at Orange, an all-in-one shopping, dining and entertainment promenade. For those looking for a more traditional shopping, the Westfield MainPlace mall is just south on Interstate 5 at Main Street. Also, the Anaheim GardenWalk, a Shoppertainment center, has some very nice restaurants, lots of shops, an upscale Bowling Center, a multi-plex theater and much more.
