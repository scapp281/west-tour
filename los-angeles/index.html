Los Angeles Introduction

It really is warm and sunny most days of the year. Summer in Los Angeles tends to be warm and dry with blue skies. Winter and spring are pleasant times to visit Los Angeles. As the home of entertainment industry, there are more artists, theatres and museums than in any other city. You can swing a smartphone and might encounter a movie star.


Sunset Boulevard is a perfect place to begin sighseeing in Los Angeles. Near by Sunset Boulevard is Sunset Strip, another celebrities's favorite haunt. It is home to the best shopping, night life and restaurants in Los Angeles. Walk of Fame has to be the most famous sidewalk in the world. The Walk of Fame pays homage to the greatest names in the entertainment industry by immortalising them in an iconic tribute star. New names are constantly added. You could be lucky enough to witness a star induction ceremony on your LA visit!


Local dishes of LA are influenced by the cuisine and culture of other countries. You could find food from all corners of the globe are readily available. As far as delicious desserts go, cheesecakes are popular in Los Angeles.
