Sea World

The country's premier entertainment center, where the performers are dolphins, otters, sea lions, orcas, and seals. Although SeaWorld is best known as the home to pirouetting dolphins and fluke-flinging killer whales, it also plays a role in rescuing and rehabilitating beached animals found along the West Coast. San Diego Introduction

San Diego is the second largest city in California and eighth largest in the United States with a population of approximately 1.3 million in 2007. There are 93 miles of shoreline including bays, lagoons and the Pacific Ocean.


The Patriot Jet Boat is the newest and most thrilling tour San Diego has to offer. So climb aboard and hold on for this exhilarating 30-minute high-speed water ride around the San Diego Bay. When guests embark on the Patriot Jet Boat, they get the chance to enjoy a truly diverse adventure and experience some heart-pounding fun.


Visitors who love beautiful views, adventure, quality time with good friends and fun are sure to have the change to experience a little bit of everything. Other than the picturesque views of famed San Diego Bay, the Patriot Jet Boat provides guests amazing sights of downtown San Diego skyline and legendary Coronado Bridge, which is a prominent attraction in the vast city.


Jet Boat is sure to provide an awesome ride for adventure seekers and people who enjoy the rush of speed with pair of 1,400 HP engines! Hold onto your seats as the highly experienced drivers behind the wheel executing spins, slides, fish tails, power slides and more exhilarating boat maneuvers.
